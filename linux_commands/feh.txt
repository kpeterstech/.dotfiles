Commands:

-f - open up a file list (.m3u)

-Y - hide pointer during a slideshow

-F - Fullscreen. Also sets unfilled background to black

-Dn - -D sets the delay between slides for a slideshow. The `n` is how many seconds between slides

Keybindings:

d - toggles filenames on top of the picture

h - pauses a slideshow

o - toggles pointer visibility (useful for presentations)

(Home) - Jump to the first image in slideshow

(End) - Jump to the last image in slideshow
